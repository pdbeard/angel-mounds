Q: Can the public visit Angel Mounds?

Yes, Angel Mounds is an Indiana State Historic Site and is open to the public. Facilities at the site include an interpretive center interpretive center, recreations of Mississippian buildings and a working reconstruction of the 1939 WPA archaeology laboratory. The 500-acre non-archaeological portion of the site contains a nature preserve with hiking and biking trails. For more information, visit: http://www.indianamuseum.org/explore/angel-mounds.
